Daily Rhythm

Imsak (end of suhoor): 10 min before Fajr

Fajr & Qur'an circle: 30 min after prayer

Dhuhr & short reminder: 12:35 PM

ʿAṣr Tafsir: 4:00 PM (daily)

Maghrib / Iftar: call to prayer, then dates & meal

ʿIshā’ & Taraweeh: 8:00 PM start

Last 10 nights: Tahajjud at 3:30 AM, I'tikaf ongoing
